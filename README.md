# Sales Analysis
### Project Overview
---
This data analysis project aims to provide insights into the sales perfomance of an e-commerce company over the past year. By analyzing various aspects of the sales data, we seek to identify trends, make data driven recommendations, and gain a deeper understanding of the company's perfomance.

### Data sources
---
Sales Data: The primary dataset used for this analysis "sales_data.csv" file, containing detailed information about each sale made by the company.

### Tools
---
- Excel - Data cleaning, analysis and creating a report [Download here](https://microsoft.com)

### Data cleaning
---
In the initial data preparation phase, we perfomed the following tasks:
1. Data Loading and inspection.
2. Handling missing values.
3. Data cleaning and Formatting.

### Exploratory Data Analysis
---
EDA involved exploring the sales data to answer key questions, such as:

- What is the overall sales trends
- What is the peak sales periods?
- Which country and region recorded the highest sales?
- What is the overall sales trends according to each store?

### Results/Findings
---
The Analysis results are summarized as follows:
1. South Africa has been leading for the 2 years (2021 and 2022) in sales.
2. When the services were discounted the sales dropped unlike when there was no discount.
3. The peak sales months were between August and september.
4. Africa and asia recorded the highest number of sales.
5. Departmental and supermarkets recorded the highest sales amongst the stores.

### Recommendations
---
Based on the analysis, we recommend the following actions:
- Invest in marketing and promotions during peak sales season to maximize revenue.
- Strategically discount to avoid decrease in sales.
- Increase supply in the high selling regions.
